Zvarevashe Kudakwashe

Chairman (Information Technology Department)

Academic Background

Kudakwashe holds an MTech in Information Technology from Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTUH, India) and a BSc in Information Systems from Midlands State University (MSU, Zimbabwe).

Experience

He has worked as a Computer Science teacher prior to joining the Harare Institute of Technology, HIT. He has more than 4 Years University experience in Teaching, Research and Administration.

Publications

He wrote 10 research papers in the areas of Security, Web Services, Cloud Computing and Big Data.

Research Interest

His areas of interest are Network Security, Security, Web Services, Cloud Computing and Big Data.

Membership

He is also a professional member of ACM.

Jekese Gladman

Lecturer

Academic Background

He is a holder of an MTech in Information Technology from SRM University, India and a BSc (Hons) in Information Systems from Midlands State University (MSU, Zimbabwe).

Experience

He has experience as a Network Administrator and ICT Technician from IBM and DELL Zimbabwe. He also has experience in research, project supervision and lecturing in Information Technology. He is certified in IBM Enterprise Technical Support for AIX and Linux-v2, IBM 000-221-AIX 7 Administration and DPES0909WBTT-Dell PowerEdge Server-Technical-v1.

Publications

He has written four research papers in fields encompassing cloud computing and security, object oriented software engineering for international publications and has also presented his researches at international conferences.

Research Interest

His areas of interest are Cloud Computing and Security, Information Communication Technologies for Development (ICT4D), Mobile Computing Technologies OO Software Engineering and SoftwareTesting

Membership
